# Platform engagement (hooks + ethical mystery)

Internal **`editor-*`** personas must follow this in addition to brand voice.

## Principles

- **Hooks:** cold open, pattern interrupt, scroll-stop in first line/first 3s (shorts).
- **Mystery / curiosity gap:** open loops that **pay off inside the same piece** — no clickbait that contradicts body.
- **Native rhythm:** X — punchy, thread/carousel hooks; LinkedIn — line breaks, authority frame + concrete takeaway; blog — H2 skim layer + in-post questions; newsletter — subject-line clarity + preview honesty; shorts — hook @0s, pattern break ~15s.

## Forbidden (dark patterns)

Fake countdowns, false limited availability, invented testimonials, moral panic framing, **stats not in `metrics-current.json`**.

## Veto

**`vp-brand`** and **`ciso-content`** may block publish until violations fixed.

## Metrics tie-in

Strength of CTA, posting cadence hints, and topic selection must align with **`kb/90-Analytics/metrics-current.json`** when present — do not claim performance you cannot cite from that file.
